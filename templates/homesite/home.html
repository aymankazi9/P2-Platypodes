Welcome the COVID-19 Tracker by the P2-Platypodes. Our goal is to provide everyone with accessible information. Our website is available worldwide and gives accurate COVID-19 statistics. We get our data from Worldometers, a reputable data source.

What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 (Coronavirus Infectious Disease - 2019) This virus directly attacks a victim's lungs and can cause respiratory related issues. The exact patterns of morbidity are still being studied. To learn more about this virus, visit our Information Page.

Medical Steps

You may be thinking, what steps are medical professionals taking to bring us closer to a COVID-19 free world. Many researchers and doctors are working long hours to bring you vaccines. The current vaccine is only an mRNA vaccine so it doesn't have the potential to infect you with COVID-19 Therefore, when you get the chance, you should get vaccinated. However, keep in mind priority groups.

What Can You Do?

Although you may not be a doctor or some researcher, you can do your part to save our planet. In fact, the main issue is people not doing their part and we're here to advocate against that. Do your part by masking up whenever your out of your home, make sure to reduce your exposure to others after you've been outside, and much more you can do. Visit our Prevention Tips page in the Information Page to see what else you can do.
